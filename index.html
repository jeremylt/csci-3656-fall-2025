This is the course website for CSCI 3656 at CU Boulder, fall 2025. This course covers development, computer implementation, and analysis of numerical methods for applied mathematical problems. It also explores topics such as floating point arithmetic, numerical solution of linear systems of equations, root finding, numerical interpolation, differentiation, and integration.
